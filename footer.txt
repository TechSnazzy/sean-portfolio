<p>I would have tried AppDeploy Repackager (snapshot) but it cannot create an MSI from another MSI.</p>
<p>How are you guys getting this to work? It seems like everyone is getting this but me.</p>

    <div class="question-answer-info">

         <div class="the-answer">Hi Blake,<br>I found some issues to with the installer.&nbsp; This is how I got around the seemingly corupt MSI.<br>go to the download page for Enterprise Ed. Select the 64bit version if needed.&nbsp; Click the download button and untick the stuff.&nbsp; When your browser has the "download" button for the MSI, don't click it.&nbsp; Wait for the page to finish loading behind the D/L request.&nbsp; Alt click the "Retry Download" button and copy the link location.&nbsp; Paste it into notepad and trim the path from the .../tag/s [delete all the stuff here] /dl/chrome...., then paste the new path in your browser.&nbsp; if you get the same page and D/L popup right away, you got it, then go for a coffee :)<br>I found the download somehow sometimes puts all that junk in the bottom of the MSI.&nbsp; if you trim the path, it doesn't go in the MSI.<br>Just what I have found from experience.<br></div>
